Family

"You don't choose your family. They are God's gift to you, as you are to them." -Desmond Tutu

My husband.

My husband's name is Gustavo Rayos. He's the love of my life. He is very sweet and kind, and keeps me motivated to do good. We met in college and he graduated from New Mexico State Universitiy in 2015. He is now a Systems Administrator in Amarillo, Texas where we live with our two dogs.

My dog Tex.

Tex is a Shih Tzu - Poodle mix and is one of the best dogs you'll ever meet. He has lots of energy and makes us laugh and happy everyday. He loves to go on trips with us and go on adventures with us when we can take him.

My dog Ranger.

Ranger is a short haired, bob-tailed Border Collie and is also a great dog. He is so strong and loves to go on walks, not the best situation for us because he pulls on the leash so hard, but it's fun anyways. Tex and Ranger are best friends and play all day together.

Hobbies

"My personal hobbies are reading, listening to music, and silence." -Edith Sitwell

I love to color.

One of my favorite things to do is to color. Coloring is very relaxing to me, and I use it to destress at the end of a long day.

Walking and talking.

I love to take walks with my family, and be able to catch up with what is going on in their lives. Walking allows me to exercise at the end of the day, and gives me time to talk to my husband and enjoy our time together. Our dogs join us to try to burn off some of their energy (which is unlimited).

Pinterest crafts.

We all know what Pinterest is, and I love Pinterest because it gives me ideas on crafts. I love being crafty and frugal and Pinterest allows me to be both. Check out my Pinterest account if you have time to see what I've been up to.

Cities

hometown

1. the town or city in which a person lives or was born, or from which a person comes.

2. of or relating to a hometown.

Las Cruces, New Mexico

Las Cruces, New Mexico is the city I was born in. I spent the first 16 years of my life there and I wouldn't have had it any other way. It is a small town, but it treated us very well and there is plenty of things to do with your friends. There are outdoor activites, sports, parks, movies, mall, etc.

Austin, Texas

When I was 16 years old, my mom, dad, and I moved to Austin, Texas. We relocated because of my dad's work. Although it was hard at first to adjust to high school again and make friends, I loved that we moved to Austin. Austin is such an amazing place and it has so many things going for it. The weather is nice, there are so many jobs, and there is so much to do ranging from outdoor activities, nightlife, music, and much more. My husband and I have made the commitment to one day get to Austin again.

Amarillo, Texas

In December of 2015, my husband graduated college and got a really good job opportunity in Amarillo, Texas. We moved to Amarillo in January 2016 and are currently still here. It is our hometown now, and it has really treated us well the past couple of years. We have been able to start our lives and careers here, and have met a lot a great people here. It will always hold a place in our hearts, even when our time in this city is done.
